Shopska Salad

Description

Shopska Salad is a traditional Bulgarian dish celebrated for its simplicity and vibrant flavors. Comprising diced tomatoes, cucumbers, and bell peppers, the salad is adorned with red onion rings and typically topped with crumbled feta cheese. Dressed with olive oil and sprinkled with salt and a dash of vinegar, Shopska Salad offers a refreshing and harmonious blend of fresh vegetables and tangy cheese. This light and colorful salad are not only a staple in Bulgarian cuisine but also a popular choice for its delightful taste and visual appeal.

Ingredients

  • Diced tomatoes
  • Diced cucumbers
  • Diced bell peppers
  • Red onion rings
  • Crumbled feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Dice tomatoes, cucumbers, and bell peppers.
  2. Arrange the diced vegetables on a serving plate.
  3. Add red onion rings on top of the vegetables.
  4. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the salad.
  5. Drizzle olive oil over the salad for dressing.
  6. Season with salt to taste.
  7. Optionally, add a dash of vinegar for extra flavor.
  8. Gently toss the salad to combine the ingredients.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy the vibrant and refreshing Shopska Salad!
